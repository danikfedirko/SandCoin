World’s first sand quarry to launch ICO

Sand Coin — the first ever sand quarry project funded through raising cryptocurrency via creating a blockchain option.

Sandcoin token is the Ethereum based token the underlying asset of which is the real product. Each Sandcoin is equal to 1 m3 of high-quality building sand.

The goal of the project is to raise funds via a blockchain system and an ICO to develop a sand quarry in the Moscow Region.

Key advantages
Already working business
The company extracts sand in the quarries of Moscow and the region since 2014.
Vast territory
Acreage of the plot in the Moscow Region is 109 hectare (269,354 acres). The total volume of sand is estimated to be 14.4 million cubic meters. Extracted and ready for sale product will consist of grade 1 and grade 2 sand.
Highly profitable
Estimated total earnings of the quarry are $76 320 000.
Convenient logistics
The land is situated 60 km away from Moscow and 9 km away from the construction site of Central Ring Road junction.

ICO details

Sandcoin token is based on Ethereum, which gives us stability and security. Each sandcoin is equal to 1 m3 of high-quality building sand. The total volume of sand quarrying is estimated to be 14.4 million cubic meters.

The average market price for 1 m3 of sand is $5.30.

The volume of sand to be sold during pre-ICO and ICO in total is 3.4 million m3 which is equal to 3 400 000 sandcoins. During the fundraising investors will be offered to buy the distributed crypto coins at a price 1 SND = $1.25 – $1.35 per 1 m3 of building sand.

The goal for the campaign is 4 000 000 $. 3 170 000 of tokens will be sold at price of 1,25-1,35$ per sandcoin

Token details

Sandcoin — blockchain-derivative with the building sand as the underlying asset.

For ICO investors we offer substantial income receipt straight after releasing sandcoin on the exchange. During the fundraising investors will be offered to purchase a blockchain option of 1 m3 sand at aprice 1 SND = $1.25 – $1.35 while the average market price of 1 m3 of sand is $5.30.

We assume a significant increase in the common-stock value of sandcoin after releasing on the stock exchange and the equalizing of the token price with the average market prices of building sand at the beginning of quarrying in May 2018.

The next stage of token price appreciation is expected through a buy-back which will take place from July 2018. Later token price appreciation will depend on integration with partner companies on the market of non-metallic materials, in February, 2019, and the beginning of the second quarry’s mining in 2020.

Through creating a blockchain-option with the real product as the underlying asset for a low price and issuing SAND COIN, which includes entering the cryptocurrency market and subsequent buy back, we offer a profitable asset to interested investors.
